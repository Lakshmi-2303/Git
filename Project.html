What is Git TechCon?

GeeksforGeeks TechCon 2025 brings together leading minds in programming, tech, and innovation. Join us for a day of insightful talks, hands-on workshops, and an opportunity to network with fellow geeks and professionals from all around the world.

Whether you're a seasoned developer, a tech enthusiast, or just starting your journey in the world of programming, Git TechCon has something valuable for everyone. Our carefully curated sessions cover the latest trends, tools, and techniques in the technology landscape.
